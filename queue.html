Real-Time Sample Queue

Real-time project/sample information is available in the queue display below. This allows you to monitor the progress of your samples through the IMR workflow. It also allows prospective clients to get a sense of how full the current queued sequencing runs are for those who wish to multiplex without contributing whole runs (as discussed on our submissions page) - this applies mostly to NextSeq runs only, as MiSeq runs are now at full capacity all the time.

Warning: Due to high demand, sample queue is quite full, extending typical data delivery time to ~3-4 months.
